Tourism News

Sharm El-Sheikh

Sharm El-Sheikh is characterized by the presence of many beautiful coral reefs that attract divers and water sports enthusiasts. The city also contains luxurious resorts and international hotels in addition to lovely restaurants and cafes.

by Leland H. Hartwell (Author), Michael L. Goldberg (Author), Janice A. Fischer (Author), Leroy Hood (Author)

New York City

Radio City is a famous entertainment complex and landmark in Midtown Manhattan, New York City. It originally opened in 1932 and contains several venues and studios. The centerpiece and most well-known venue is Radio City Music Hall. With its Art Deco style interior decor featuring a large proscenium arch stage and red velour seats, the Music Hall hosts concerts, stage shows, awards shows, and more.

by Leland H. Hartwell (Author), Michael L. Goldberg (Author), Janice A. Fischer (Author), Leroy Hood (Author)

London,England

Packed with over 300 shops, Oxford Street is Europe's busiest shopping street. It features numerous luxury department stores, flagship retail stores, high-end boutiques, restaurants, cafes and movie theaters. Historic landmarks located on Oxford Street include the world-famous Selfridges department store, the equally iconic Marble Arch monument and the stunning St. Mary's Church.

by Leland H. Hartwell (Author), Michael L. Goldberg (Author), Janice A. Fischer (Author), Leroy Hood (Author)

Bidoup Nui Ba National Park,Vietnam

Bidoup Nui Ba National Park is located in the south-central highlands of Vietnam. It is characterized by its beautiful mountainous terrain and dense vegetation cover. The park encompasses a huge area of over 200,000 hectares. Bidoup Nui Ba has a diverse ecosystem with both evergreen and coniferous forests. Some of the trees found here include pine, bamboo, rattan palm, and rosewood. The fauna is also varied, with Asian black bears, sun bears, muntjac deer, and even rare primates like the red-shanked douc and black-shanked douc.

by Leland H. Hartwell (Author), Michael L. Goldberg (Author), Janice A. Fischer (Author), Leroy Hood (Author)
